Lattice Animal Generation



All neighbors of a generic central cell.

The maximal number of fixed are generated by breadth first searching a neighbor tiling. Restricted cells prevent duplicate shapes from being generated. As the algorithm progresses, red tracks the current exploration path. Cells are made available and unavailable as shapes are completed and the algorithm recurses back and follows another branch. Blue marks the neighbors of the current cell which are added according to the tiling and the current availability.
